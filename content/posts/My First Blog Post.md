Hey 👋🏽 **I’m Tradix — tech lover, code enthusiast, and someone who’s been dreaming for a while about building something of my own.**  

I’ve had a website before ([tradixthecompany.com](https://www.tradixthecompany.com)), but after a *very* bumpy start, I decided it was time for a clean slate. Enter: [tradix.dev](https://www.tradix.dev).  

This blog post is different from my first attempt. For starters — I’m writing it in **Obsidian**. Why? Because I wanted a simple, seamless workflow for blogging, and Obsidian just fits.  

But there’s a bigger idea here.  
This isn’t just “another blog” — I want it to be the first chapter in a concept I’m calling **WIN**.  
It’s a place for people to document their journey — from *grass to grace*.  

Will it work out? Who knows. But it would be awesome if it did.  
And if you’ve been sitting on an idea of your own — take this as your push to start. No matter how small.  

---

Back in March (my last web deployment), I had big aspirations.  
But I also hit big roadblocks — from *skill gaps* to *mental battles*.  
Those challenges forced me to rethink how I approach life, and how I see the whole “winning” process.  

I’ve learned a lot between March and now, and I plan to weave those lessons into everything I post here. Stick around — you might just pick up something useful along the way.

## 🧠 Mindset Going In

I know two things for sure:  
1. **A boring blog won’t last.**  
2. **You can’t serve everyone.**  

This blog is for the people chasing *more* — whether it’s a dream career, financial freedom, or better health.  
The part that most people never see is the *process*.  
The messy middle. The struggle before the win. That’s what I’m here to share.  

I want to get better at writing — at keeping your attention, delivering value, and making the story worth your time. Even if no one reads in the beginning, I’ll keep posting.  

Because I’m here to stay. And I won’t stop until I’ve built the life I’m aiming for.  

Thanks for reading my first post. This is just the start. 🚀 (Updated 08-08-2025)
